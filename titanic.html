Titanic Gendarmenmarkt Berlin

Welcome to Titanic Gendarmenmarkt Berlin

This luxurious hotel features a grill restaurant and a spa with an authentic Turkish hammam/steam room. Titanic Gendarmenmarkt is located in the heart of Berlin, just 200 meters from the elegant Gendarmenmarkt. All rooms at the Titanic Gendarmenmarkt Berlin are air-conditioned and beautifully furnished. A rich breakfast buffet is served every morning at the hotel. In the Beef Grill Club by Hasir with show kitchen, meals are prepared in front of you. Meeting facilities, luggage storage and dry cleaning are also available at the hotel. Titanic Gendarmenmarkt Berlin is a 5-minute walk from the lively Friedrichstrasse and the famous Unter den Linden boulevard. The nearby Hausvogteiplatz and Museumsinsel subway stations offer excellent transport connections to all of Berlin
