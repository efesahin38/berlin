Steigenberger Hotel Am Chancellery

Welcome to Steigenberger Hotel Am Chancellery

Just 800 meters from the Brandenburg Gate, the Steigenberger Hotel Am Kanzleramt is right next to the German Parliament buildings in Berlin. WiFi is available free of charge. Each air-conditioned room at the Steigenberger Hotel Am Kanzleramt features a minibar, tea/coffee making facilities and a flat-screen TV. The private bathroom includes bathrobes and slippers. The rooms are modernly designed. Facilities at the Steigenberger Hotel Am Kanzleramt include a 24-hour reception, a bar and a restaurant. The hotel also offers ticket service and luggage storage. Alexanderplatz is 3 km from the hotel, Kurfürstendamm is 4 km away and Potsdamer Platz is a 25-minute walk away. Berlin Central Station is right next to the hotel and Berlin-Schönefeld Airport is 25 km away.
