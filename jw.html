JW Marriott Hotel Berlin

Welcome to JW Marriott Hotel Berlin

This First Class hotel is located just 1 km from Potsdamer Platz, next to Tiergarten Park. The hotel offers free WiFi and a swimming pool. The rooms at JW Marriott Hotel Berlin are elegantly decorated and each features a marble bathroom. Each room is equipped with air conditioning, a multimedia smart TV, a kettle for coffee and tea, and a laptop safe. You can enjoy the swimming pool, sauna, steam room, and relaxation area in the healthy living center, or exercise in the fitness center. The Market restaurant serves a rich buffet breakfast with regional and international options. At the terrace of The Steakhouse restaurant, you can enjoy quality steaks processed in Dry Ager. Bar26 offers cocktails, alcoholic beverages, and snacks on its terrace. Potsdamer Platz, Sony Center, Reichstag building, and the green oasis "Tiergarten" park are within walking distance of many famous tourist attractions.
